About

Hair Day is a boutique hair salon based in Los Angeles, California. We specialize in precision cuts, dimensional color, and modern styling, with a strong focus on bold expression and effortlessly elevated looks. Inspired by the creativity and confidence of the West Coast, we blend technical expertise with artistic vision to create styles that feel both current and timeless.

Due to the detailed nature of our coloring services, we accept a limited number of appointments each week to ensure every client receives personalized attention and exceptional care. At Hair Day, we believe great hair is more than a look — it's a statement. Whether you're going for subtle refinement or a dramatic transformation, our goal is to make you leave feeling confident, refreshed, and completely yourself.
